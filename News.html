A new James Peter Cost Originals Added


“Pacific” is a James Peter Cost original.  It oil on canvas and measures 24” x 48.”  


This painting currently is in an estate and has been offered for resale.  It was just restored, cleaned, and revarnished by Shelley Cost, the official restorer of her dad’s work.

Three New Shelley Cost Paintings


“Inspiration Near Angel’s Camp” is a 6”x9” opaque watercolor, and is glassed.  This image is an exquisite painting of California’s Mother Lode.

Before restoration:

“Carmel Valley Reflections” is a 12x16” oil painting on acid free board.  It was painted in 1966 and is of Carmel Valley, California.

“All Doors are Open” is a 6x8” mixed media on acid-free board.  It was sketched on location in Salinas Valley, California, in 1979.
