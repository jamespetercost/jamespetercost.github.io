Original Works of Art by James Peter Cost

Paintings for resale were originally purchased from BettyJo Cost at the James Peter Cost Gallery.  Information on the gallery may be found on the page, About this Site.  Most resales are in need of restoration. The paintings in need of sealing, cleaning and re-varnishing are first shipped to the Monterey studio of Shelley Cost—the official restorer of JPC’s paintings.  Once restored they are returned to Harrod’s of Newport to be shown on this website for resale.
