Original Works of Art by James Peter Cost

Paintings for resale were originally purchased from BettyJo Cost at the James Peter Cost Gallery.  Information on the gallery may be found on the About page.  Most resales are in need of restoration.  Those paintings in need of re-sealing, cleaning and re-varnishing are first shipped to the Monterey studio of Shelley Cost—the official restorer of JPC’s paintings.  Once restored they are returned to Harrod’s of Newport for resale.
