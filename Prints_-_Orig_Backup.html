Reproductions of Original Works of Art by

James Peter Cost

The print of “The Yellow Truck” is a Collotype, and was printed in New York by Triton Press.  With the exception of the Mobil Gas Pump, “Fill-er-up”, which is a Serigraph on canvas, all other prints are Lithographs reproduced on paper.  Those on paper have to be mounted before framing due to the many years of storage.
